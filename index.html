COMIC BOOK APPARENCES
Scott William Pilgrim is the titular protagonist of the Scott Pilgrim media franchise. He is the bassist of the band Sex Bob-omb, at his 22 years begins a relationship with schoolgirl Knives Chau, which is disapproved of by most of his friends. Scott meets an American girl, Ramona Flowers, who has been appearing in his dreams and becomes an obsession with him, losing interest in Knives. While competing in a battle of the bands whose prize is a record deal with the enigmatic G-Man, Scott is attacked by Matthew Patel, who introduces himself as Ramona's "first evil ex-boyfriend." Scott manages to defeat him and learns that in order to win his stability with Ramona, he will have to face all seven of his exes.
